Fridge Poems

Poems need fridges! Fridges need poems!

Start making your own unique fridge poem by providing your own text below (the more text the better!). We'll make word magnets for you using a random selection from the text you provide when you click the 'Select 50 Words' button. Or... if you'd rather play with words from a curated library, use the 'Get 50 Classic Words' button below!

Your word magnets will show up below. Drag them around to make your very own fridge poem! To throw away a magnet, drag it out the bottom of the screen. To start fresh, click the 'Fridge Reset' button. To save your fridge poem, click the 'Save as Image' button at the bottom of the page.
